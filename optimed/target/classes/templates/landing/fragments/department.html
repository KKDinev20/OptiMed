Our Departments

Cardiology

Our cardiology department specializes in heart and blood vessel disorders. Our team of cardiologists provides comprehensive care for conditions ranging from hypertension to complex cardiac procedures.

Oncology

Our oncology department offers advanced cancer treatment options, including chemotherapy, radiation therapy, and surgical interventions. Our multidisciplinary team works together to create personalized treatment plans.

Neurology

Our neurology department diagnoses and treats disorders of the brain, spinal cord, and nervous system. From migraines to complex neurological conditions, our specialists provide expert care.

Pediatrics

Our pediatric department provides comprehensive medical care for infants, children, and adolescents. Our child-friendly facilities and experienced pediatricians ensure the best possible care for young patients.
